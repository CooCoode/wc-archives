DAO Space Network: 

Share your Space with SeeDAO 

to co-build a Post-Paradise for Builders

Remote work has become a trend after the pandemic. Though remote work provides many benefits, face-to-face interaction is still necessary for people.

The rise of shared spaces has provided many startups with their first workspace. These open spaces allow communities to communicate, collaborate, and co-create. Gradually, offline spaces are becoming a trend.

The usage change of shared space is just like magic. During the daytime, the space could be the study room. After sunset, it becomes a movie screening room or a popular bar. When people want to create or share something, it could also work as an exhibition hall or a hacker house.

Dear space owners, are your offices, meeting rooms, cafes, galleries, or other spaces often vacant? Are you also excited about bringing magic to these venues?

We believe that space is an invaluable resource with unlimited potential. Through sharing, they can become hubs that connect communities and bridges between people. Encouraging gatherings for experts to exchange ideas? Having a relaxed and enjoyable roundtable discussion? Or a Web3 Builders meetup? Space has unlimited potential when shared with creative people.

If you bring the beer, we'll bring the cheer! If you have space, we have a global network of exceptional Web3 and digital nomad sharers.

The SeeDAO community currently has over 10,000 members, with members spanning across Asia, Africa, Latin America, Europe, America, and Oceania. SeeDAO's vision is to establish a digital city-state that connects one million individuals. We believe in the power of online networks and firmly acknowledge the equal importance of every offline activity in building networks.

The City Connectors program aims to establish a real-world network for people. In April this year, we organized various activities in 10 cities worldwide, expanding SeeDAO's network in different global cities. Moreover, we have also hosted DAO conferences such as DAO Hong Kong and DAO Montenegro, connecting numerous partners around the world.

During the implementation of DAO Space Network, we have established a partnership with the digital nomad community in Dali, specifically with DaliHub. Recently, we have also been in contact with MetaSpace in Beijing and Time-Space Community in Huzhou. In the future, digital nomads, artists, planners, project managers, and other roles within SeeDAO will bring interesting and diverse changes in these spaces.

We believe that every space has the potential to become the core of offline interpersonal connections.

We look forward to working with partners worldwide to build a more open and inclusive community space. This space will be accessible to nomadic crypto enthusiasts around the globe. It will unlock significant business, culture, and life potential, connecting the digital and physical worlds and creating a human-centered network. 

If you want to bring more possibilities to your own space or make your space a node in the real-world network, you can't miss the DAO Space Network Project.

If you want to share the following space with more people, join us now:

-Shared living spaces and workspaces for digital nomads.

-Web3-friendly cultural spaces, such as cafes and bookstores.

-Web3-friendly event venues include offices, galleries, art museums, and auditoriums.

-Web3-friendly villages or micro-cities (Then we can co-host larger-scale living experiments.)

As a co-builder of space, you could:

1.Become a partner of SeeDAO: SeeDAO would help you promote the location and the activities, including providing posters, putting your venues on the official website of SeeDAO, and getting promoted on social media.

2.Become a hub of SeeDAO's network: Communicate with more builders face to face, talk with more content creators, and gradually become a base for numerous participants.
